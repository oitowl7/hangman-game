If you don't know how to play Cut-Head-Off-Man, it's quite simple. The purpose of the game is to not have Ned's head fall off. In order to do this, you have to guess the house that is being offered. If you do, congradulations! You just saved Ned. If not...well.......basically it's your fault his head fell off. Hope you can live with yourself.

For greatest enjoyment/guilt, play with the music in the background.

In order to start the game, press the start button. The number of Neds that you have saved will be tracked. More importantly, the number of Neds that you have killed will also be tracked. You should try to keep this number at 0 because we all know that there is only one true Ned. May God have mercy on your soul.
